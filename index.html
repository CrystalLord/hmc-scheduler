Scheduler (1 of 1)

Here's a tool to help you arrange your classes by showing every possible schedule. Here's how to use it:
  1. Drag this Scheduler bookmarklet to your bookmarks
  2. Do a search on Portal for the classes you want to take
  3. Click the Scheduler bookmarklet while on the results page
  4. Add every section of a class using the "schedule" button
  5. Repeat for every class you're interested in
  6. Now click Generate to see every possible schedule
  7. Save/print the ones you like!
You can also manually edit course titles, times, and colors by clicking its course title.
