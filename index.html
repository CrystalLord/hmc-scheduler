Scheduler (1 of 1) (no schedules possible)

Here's a tool to help you arrange your classes by showing every possible schedule. Here's how to use it:
  1. Drag this Scheduler bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar
  2. Search on Portal for the classes you want to take
  3. Click the Scheduler bookmarklet while on the search results
  4. Add all the sections of classes you like
  5. Navigate possible schedules using the arrows above
  6. Save/print the ones you like!
You can also manually edit course titles, times, and colors by clicking its course title.
